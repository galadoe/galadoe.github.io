About Me

Galadoe Kulee is a student at Iowa State University School of Engineering, pursing a degree in Software Engineering. She has taken a semster off to attend a programming bootcamp (LearnAcademy), in hopes of gaining practical experience in programming. Her fascination with computing (programming) started when she was in junior high school. This fascination led her to learning to code on her own.

As an immigrant and a woman of color, she comes from a community that is under represented in the STEM fields. She's looking forward to being part of a new generation of black women in the STEM space that will start to change this narrative.

Galadoe is a team player, she's very passionate about working with others to achieve overall institutioal objectives. While the technology space is rapidly changing and advancing, she wants to continue learning to stay up to date with new technologies.
